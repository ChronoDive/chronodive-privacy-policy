ChronoDive Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

ChronoDive is an editorial video discovery app. This policy explains what the app stores, what third-party services are involved, and what users can expect today.

Information ChronoDive stores

ChronoDive currently stores a small amount of app data locally on the device to support core functionality. This may include your selected year, saved moments, and resume position in a feed.

Video playback and YouTube

ChronoDive uses YouTube API Services and the YouTube embedded player to play videos. When you play a video, YouTube and Google may collect and process data according to their own terms and privacy policies.

  • YouTube Terms of Service: https://www.youtube.com/t/terms
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

What ChronoDive does not do

  • ChronoDive does not download YouTube videos.
  • ChronoDive does not extract or rehost video files.
  • ChronoDive does not create a separate media copy outside the YouTube embedded player.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to chronodive.support@gmail.com.

Updates

This policy may be updated as ChronoDive evolves, especially if account features, analytics, or additional data handling are added.
